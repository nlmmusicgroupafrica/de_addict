Biography

De_Addict is a deep house and deep tech artist known for hypnotic grooves, rolling basslines, and immersive atmospheres. His sound draws from underground electronic textures, blending soulful depth with raw, rhythmic energy. Signed to NLM Music Group Africa, he continues to push the boundaries of African-influenced electronic music.
